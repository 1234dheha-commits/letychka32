What it is

Nearby, private, offline.

Letychka uses Bluetooth to find other people running the app close to you. Everything stays between your phones.

No internet, no servers

Messages travel directly between phones over Bluetooth LE. There is no backend and no cloud. Nothing is routed through us.

Anonymous

No account, no phone number, no email. Just a nickname you choose. Chats live only while the app is open and then they are gone.

Radar

People nearby appear as blips. Tap one to start chatting.

Text and small media

Send messages, tiny photos and short voice notes over Bluetooth.

Yours to control

Delete or edit messages, clear everything on your phone anytime.

Honest about Bluetooth

Letychka only works with people physically near you (roughly 10 to 30 meters, less through walls), with the app open on both phones and Bluetooth on. It is a local, offline tool, not a replacement for internet messengers. Media is small and compressed because Bluetooth is slow.
